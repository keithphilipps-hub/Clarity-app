About Dr. Clarity
Dr. Clarity draws on established, evidence-based frameworks:
· Gottman Method (John & Julie Gottman) — conflict patterns, Four Horsemen, repair
· Emotionally Focused Therapy (Dr. Sue Johnson) — attachment cycles in couples
· Attachment Theory (Bowlby & Ainsworth) — how early bonds shape adult relationships
· CBT (Aaron Beck) — identifying and challenging cognitive distortions
· Nonviolent Communication (Marshall Rosenberg) — needs-based communication
· DBT Skills (Marsha Linehan) — emotional regulation and distress tolerance
· Window of Tolerance (Dr. Dan Siegel) — nervous system awareness

Dr. Clarity is AI, not a licensed therapist. It will never guess, invent statistics, or make diagnoses. For serious mental health concerns, please seek a licensed professional.
